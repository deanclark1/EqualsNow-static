A feminist collective working on gender equality and women empowerment.

Our primary focus is learning, movement building and digital activism to advance the rights of women & girls, non-binary people and other minority groups.

Our Values

Equality

Equality for all is our driving force and is enshrined in all the work we do. Let us know how you fight for equality of the sexes

Diversity

We believe in the empowerment of all women to enhance sustainable development.

Inclusion

We work with and include women from all marginalized groups in our advocacy and programs as well as in our core team

Collaboration

Our advocacy topics range from sex, media, politics, economics, empowerment and other feminist issues being pushed forward by our team of diverse young women in The Gambia using different traditional and modern tools

Self Care

Our core theme at Equals Now is building a strong movement of Gambian feminist that are able to advocate and successfully lobby for positive change in The Gambia. This movement is open to all women and seeks to provide access to feminist spaces for all women in The Gambia
